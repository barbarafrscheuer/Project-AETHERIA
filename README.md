### üíñ **Gameplay & Learning Mechanics**

The game transforms language lessons into rewarding relationship milestones:

* **Dialogue Decisions:** Conversations are not just about choosing the right tone; they require selecting the **correct linguistic phrase or word** in the target language to earn affection points.
* **Integrated Spaced Repetition (ANKI-like):** We are building a sophisticated, in-game flashcard system. This system will dynamically generate and review vocabulary/grammar based on your conversation history and the needs of upcoming dates, making review sessions feel like preparing for a big romantic event!
* **Kanji/Kana/Hanja Challenge:** Dedicated mini-games featuring handwriting recognition (using a built-in canvas or external API) will require players to correctly draw characters to unlock special character stories or romantic CGs.
* **Affection = Proficiency:** The love level of the characters acts as the ultimate progress bar. **Your relationship cannot advance without proven language mastery.**

### üéØ **The Goal**

To prove that learning a new language doesn't have to feel like a chore. Project AETHERIA aims to create a highly motivating, fun, and emotionally engaging environment where players **want** to learn the language to progress their relationships and unlock deeper connections with their love interests.
